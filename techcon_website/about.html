Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts eager to share ideas and showcase innovations. Over the years, it has evolved into an international event that attracts thousands of attendees from around the globe.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire and empower the next generation of innovators. We believe in fostering collaboration, sharing knowledge, and driving positive change through technology.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the brightest minds in technology. Here are a few of our inspiring past speakers:

Grace Hopper

A pioneer in computer programming, Grace inspired attendees with her groundbreaking work in early programming languages and advocacy for women in technology.

Alan Turing

Known as the father of computer science, Alan's legacy in artificial intelligence and code-breaking continues to shape the modern tech landscape.

Ada Lovelace

Celebrated as the first computer programmer, Ada shared her vision of the limitless potential of computing, inspiring future innovators.
